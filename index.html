Beautiful Teriberka

Pure north

Teriberka is the only place in our country where you can see the open Arctic Ocean. Here you can try to fish and crabs, and if you get lucky, you can even see whales.

Ask Nature

Teriberka has been holding the «Teriberka. New Life», where musicians perform, open exhibitions and just have fun. Since the movie Leviathan, the village has become a popular tourist destination.
